<h2>About Me</h2>

<p style="font-family:times;font-size:18px">
I am a senior undergraduate from Southern University of Science and Technology, majoring in Robotics Engineering and advised by Prof. Wei Zhang.
I had a great research experience working in the wheeled-bipedal robot group of CLEAR Lab.</p>

<p style="font-family:times;font-size:18px">
My research interests lie in Robot Perception and Autonomous Control, especially on vision aided dynamical system control using optimization and learning.</p>
