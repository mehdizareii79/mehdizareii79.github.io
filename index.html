About Me

I am actively seeking a PhD position in Fall 2024!
Feel free to contact at yulunz@umich.edu if you are interested.

I am a master student of Robotics Department at University of Michigan (UMich), where I work on legged robot control and learning with professor Maani Ghaffari and previously on racecar autonomous racing. Prior to that, I received my bachelor degree from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), with a major in Robotics Engineering. Advised by professor Wei Zhang, I had a great research experience working on MPC control and reinforcement learning (RL) of quadruped robots.

I am strongly interested in agile legged locomotion in dynamic environments, with a focus on control and learning methods. Apart from my professional endeavors, I have a deep passion for competitive programming and tinkering small DIY projects. When I'm not immersed in technology, you can find me swimming or playing badminton.
