About Me

I am a graduate student at University of Michigan, majoring in Robotics. I earned my bachelor degree from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), majoring in Robotics Engineering and supervised by Prof. Wei Zhang. I had a great research experience working on legged robot.

My research interests lie in control and learning algorithms, with applications in mobile robots locomotion. Currently I work as a Machine Learning Engineer in the ROAHM lab supervised by Prof. Ram Vasudevan.
