About Me

I am a master student of Robotics Department at University of Michigan (UMich), where I work on legged robot control and learning with Maani Ghaffari and previously on racecar autonomous racing. Prior to that, I received my bachelor degree from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), with a major in Robotics Engineering. Advised by Prof. Wei Zhang, I had a great research experience working on MPC control and reinforcement learning (RL) of quadruped robots.

I am broadly interested in robotics perception, planning and control, with a focus on developing efficient and deployable algorithms for legged robots.

Apart from my professional endeavors, I have a deep passion for competitive programming, robotics and enjoy tinkering with small DIY projects. When I'm not immersed in technology, you can find me swimming or in badminton court.
