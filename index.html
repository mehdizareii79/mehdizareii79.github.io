About Me

I am a senior undergraduate from Southern University of Science and Technology, majoring in Robotics Engineering and advised by Prof. Wei Zhang. I had a great research experience working in the wheeled-bipedal robot group of CLEAR Lab.

My research interests lie in Robot Perception and Autonomous Control, especially on vision aided dynamical system control using optimization and learning.

NMT with Transformer on Multi30K
[Code]
Implemented a Transformer architecture to realize a full attention neural network that learns to translate German to English. The best model gains a BLEU score up to 37.39, when the minimum frequency of words is selected to be 3.

HCI Gesture Control
[Code]
Used the watershed algorithm to realize skin color image segmentation, implemented gesture recognition by template matching, and implemented the gesture interaction with mouse and keyboard based on Win32 API.

Flappy Bird Controller
[Code]
Developed an interactive controller app through MATLAB app designer to play the game autonomously. The control algorithm is implemented by bang-bang control and PID control. Besides, all controller’s parameters can be tuned in app’s interface and the trajectory of bird and tubes can be drawn in real time.

Tractor Autonomous Steering Simulation
[Code]
Developed a medium-fidelity model of two tractors in Webots and implemented the tractor model and the control algorithm in Webots, including four-wheel steering capability, body suspension and sensors. This project is collaborated with SUSTech, ND and AgJunction Inc.

Segway Locomotion
[Code]
This project is the locomotion control for a Segway robot with STM32F1. We implemented balance control, speed control, steering control method for the Segway robot together with the Bluetooth debugging function.

Fast Photo Mosaic
[Code]
Designed a feature descriptor based on the mean histogram of the LAB color space, applied the K-D tree to match the color blocks of the target image sub-region, and used the pre-computed feature pool to optimize the synthesis speed, and realized the mosaic photo that has better performance than Foto-Mosaik-Edda.
